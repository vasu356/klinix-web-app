Design a modern, high-converting SaaS landing page for "Klinix" — an AI-powered patient intake platform for dental clinics.

PRODUCT POSITIONING:Klinix replaces clipboard paperwork at dental offices with an AI-driven digital intake flow. Patients fill out forms on their phone before they arrive; AI flags medical history red flags (drug allergies, contraindications, anxiety markers); and the data syncs directly into the clinic's practice management software. The pitch is: "Stop losing 40 minutes per new patient to paperwork. Let AI handle intake while your staff handles patients."

TARGET CUSTOMER:Dental clinic owners and office managers in the US, age 35-55. They are NOT tech early adopters. They need to feel this is safe, HIPAA-compliant, and easy for their elderly front-desk staff to use. The tone should be confident and clinical, not playful or "startup-y."

VISUAL DIRECTION:

Premium B2B SaaS aesthetic. Reference quality bar: Linear (linear.app), Attio (attio.com), and Notion's marketing pages. Clean, lots of whitespace, restrained.

NOT this: bright purple gradients, illustrated mascot characters, "fun" rounded everything, cartoon doctors.

Color palette: off-white background (#FAFAF7 or similar warm white), near-black primary text (#0A0A0A), and ONE accent color — a confident medical teal (#0E7C7B) or deep ocean blue (#1E40AF). Pick one and use it consistently for CTAs, links, and one or two highlight elements. No gradient meshes.

Typography: Inter or Geist for everything, OR a pairing of Instrument Serif for headlines + Inter for body. Tight tracking on headlines, generous line-height on body.

Lots of breathing room between sections — minimum 120px vertical padding on desktop section breaks.

One subtle visual texture (very faint grain overlay or single soft gradient blob behind the hero) so it doesn't feel sterile.
